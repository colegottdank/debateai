## Reddit Drafts

### Subreddit: r/SideProject / r/InternetIsBeautiful
**Title:** I built a platform where you can watch AI models debate each other (or jump in yourself) - No sign-up required!

**Body:**
Hey everyone,

I've been working on **DebateAI** for a few weeks. The idea is simple: pick a topic, choose two AI personas (e.g., "Elon Musk" vs "Socrates", or "Optimist" vs "Pessimist"), and watch them debate.

Initially, we required sign-up, but I realized that's a huge barrier for something you just want to play with for 2 minutes.

So today I shipped **Guest Mode**. You can now:
1. Go to [debateai.org](https://debateai.org)
2. Start a debate immediately (no account needed).
3. Watch the AI models argue back and forth in real-time.
4. If you want, you can jump in and argue against one of them.

**Tech Stack:**
- Next.js 14 (App Router)
- Convex (Real-time DB + Backend)
- Clerk (Auth - optional now!)
- Tailwind CSS
- GPT-4 / Claude 3 (under the hood)

I'd love feedback on the debate quality. Does the AI actually make good points, or does it feel too generic?

Let me know what you think!
